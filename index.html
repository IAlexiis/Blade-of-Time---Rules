Blade of time - Rules

Game rules :

Setup :

  • Each player takes a pawn and a die.
  • Roll the dice: the player with the highest number starts the game.
  • Shuffle the Item cards and Event cards into two separate decks.

How a turn works :

On each turn, a player:

  • 1. Rolls the die and moves forward on the board.
  • 2. Depending on where they land, they will either:
    • Draw an Item card (useful objects),
    • Draw an Event card (unexpected events).

The cards :

Items cards :

Item cards represent valuable tools found during the adventure. They can :

  • Counter certain dangerous events.
  • Grant strategic advantages.
  • Example: Cancel the effects of a weather event or free yourself from a trapped space.

Event cards :

Event cards introduce unexpected challenges that disrupt your journey. They may:

  • Slow you down (such as being stuck for several turns).
  • Move your pawn to another location.
  • Allow you to draw a clue card.

Some events can be neutralized with the right Item card.

Bonus cards :

Special bonus cards, like the "All-Risks Insurance" card, allow you to:

Automatically protect yourself against specific events (such as adverse weather).

The Treasure and the Clues :

The treasure is hidden somewhere in the twin island archipelago.

There are 4 clue booklets, each pointing to different possible locations.

Each booklet contains 3 clues.

  • Clues can be collected by drawing certain Event cards or reaching key points on the board.
  • Gathering and interpreting clues is essential to finding the exact location of the treasure.
  • Important: Only one of the clue booklets points to the real treasure. Be wary of false leads.

Navigation on the board :

  • The board contains multiple routes leading through the islands.
  • As you progress, you will gather clues that help you choose the correct route.
  • You may encounter various challenges and opportunities as you move, making it essential to pay attention to every detail of your journey.
  • Pro Tip: If you think you're on the wrong path, you can always retrace your steps and change routes. Just keep rolling the die and advancing at your own pace, adjusting your path based on the clues you uncover.

End of the game

The first player to successfully locate the treasure using the clues wins the game and returns to their time."
